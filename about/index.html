About Me

Bio

I’m a passionate Fullstack Developer specializing in React and Node.js, with a strong interest in building efficient, user-friendly, and scalable web applications. I enjoy turning ideas into functional products and continuously exploring better ways to solve problems through clean and modern code.

Goals in This Program

My goal is to become a more well-rounded and confident developer, refining both my frontend and backend skills. I aim to strengthen my problem-solving abilities, master new technologies, and contribute to real-world projects that push me beyond my comfort zone.

Areas of Low Confidence

One of my current challenges lies in effectively using SQL databases. While I’m comfortable with NoSQL systems like MongoDB, I’m working to deepen my understanding of relational database concepts, query optimization, and schema design.
